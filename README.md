Lifestyle vs GPA Analysis Overview This project analyzes how lifestyle factors affect academic performance (GPA).
Objectives: Study the relationship between study hours and GPA Analyze the effect of sleep on performance Examine stress, physical activity, and social life.
Dataset Source: Kaggle Size: 2000 student samples.
Key Findings: Study hours have a strong positive correlation with GPA Sleep has a moderate positive effect Stress shows a complex relationship (can increase performance).
Conclusion: Lifestyle habits significantly impact academic performance.
